# speakfree

**Hold a key, speak, release.** Your words appear at the cursor — transcribed locally on your Mac by Whisper, with no internet, no account, and no subscription.

[Download for macOS →](https://github.com/definitelyreal/speakfree/releases/latest){: .btn}

---

## How it works

1. Hold the **Globe key** (🌐)
2. Speak naturally
3. Release — your words are typed instantly

Works in any app: notes, email, code editors, chat, documents.

## Completely private

Every word is transcribed on your device using [Whisper](https://github.com/openai/whisper) by OpenAI. Audio never leaves your Mac. No cloud, no logging, no data collection.

## Free, open source

speakfree is free forever. [Source on GitHub.](https://github.com/definitelyreal/speakfree)
