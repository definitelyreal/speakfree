1.
Hold the Globe key
Replaces the built-in macOS dictation shortcut
2.
Speak naturally
speakfree listens and transcribes in real time
3.
Release — your words appear
Text is typed at your cursor, in any app
🔒
Completely private
Transcribed on-device using Whisper by OpenAI. Nothing leaves your Mac. Whisper is bundled in the download — no internet required after install.
📦
Zero setup
Drag to Applications, right-click → Open. No terminal, no Homebrew, no config files. The model downloads on first launch.
⚡
Works everywhere
Dictate into any app — notes, email, Slack, code editors, documents. Wherever your cursor is, your words go. Blazing fast on Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3); also runs on Intel Macs.
